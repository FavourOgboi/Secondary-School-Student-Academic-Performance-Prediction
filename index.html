Project Overview

Our project's main goal is to determine the obstacles that students encounter and how they hinder their success on important exams like the WASSCE and JAMB. We acknowledge that pupils' achievement on regular term assessments held in schools has a significant impact on their chances of succeeding on these important tests.

In order to tackle these broad issues, we carried out an extensive survey in several schools to collect information about the typical difficulties encountered by pupils. Our goal is to examine this data and create practical plans to improve the general performance of students on regular tests and important exams.

Analysis Areas Affecting Student Performance

Demographics and Background Analysis

This analysis examines the demographic characteristics and socio-economic backgrounds of students. Understanding these factors helps in identifying patterns and trends that may affect educational outcomes.

Academic Performance Analysis

This area focuses on evaluating students' academic achievements and identifying key factors influencing their success in examinations. It encompasses performance metrics from regular assessments and standardized tests.

School Attendance and Extracurricular Activities Analysis

Here, we analyze the correlation between school attendance rates and participation in extracurricular activities. This includes assessing how these factors contribute to overall student engagement and performance.

Psychological and Social Factors Analysis

This section explores the psychological and social influences on student performance. It considers aspects such as mental health, peer relationships, and familial support, which can significantly impact academic success.

Study Habits and Support Analysis

This area evaluates students' study habits, learning strategies, and the availability of academic support systems. It aims to identify effective practices that enhance learning and performance.

Parental Involvement Analysis

This analysis examines the role of parental involvement in student academic performance, exploring how engagement at home can positively or negatively affect learning outcomes.
